# Customer Research Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-11

## Research Goals

1. Validate whether users describe the pain as "아이디 노출", "닉네임 중복", "사칭 계정", "디지털 풋프린트", or another phrase.
2. Find which segment converts first: individuals, creators, small brands, or agencies.
3. Identify objections around privacy, accuracy, and paid report value.
4. Collect safe, non-sensitive voice-of-customer language for landing pages and guide pages.

## Minimum Viable Research

Before a broad launch, collect at least:

- 5 individual user interviews.
- 5 creator/community operator interviews.
- 5 founder/small-brand interviews.
- 3 agency/operator interviews, if B2B use appears promising.

## Interview Questions

1. When was the last time you checked whether a username, nickname, or brand handle was already being used?
2. What triggered that check?
3. How did you do it manually?
4. What was annoying or uncertain about the process?
5. What would make a report trustworthy enough to act on?
6. What would make this product feel unsafe or creepy?
7. If you got a score and preview for free, what would make a paid detailed report worth it?
8. Which phrase feels most natural: 아이디 노출 점검, 닉네임 검색, 디지털 풋프린트 점검, 사칭 계정 후보 확인, 브랜드명 계정 확인?

## Online Research Sources

- Korean creator/founder communities where self-promotion is allowed.
- Reddit/Indie Hackers/Product Hunt discussions about username availability, impersonation, account squatting, and brand handles.
- App store reviews of privacy/security utilities and username checker apps.
- Support/community pages for social platform impersonation and username claim workflows.

## What Not To Collect

- Real usernames tied to identifiable people without consent.
- Sensitive account URLs from user scans.
- Real names, phone numbers, emails, locations, profile photos, or post content.

## How Research Feeds Marketing

- Copywriting: use the words users choose for the problem.
- CRO: answer objections before report unlock.
- SEO: prioritize guide page keywords that users say naturally.
- Cold email: personalize around launch/brand/creator signals, not private scan results.

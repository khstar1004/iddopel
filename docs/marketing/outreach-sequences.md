# Outreach Sequences

Last updated: 2026-06-11

Use these only for human-reviewed, low-volume outreach. Do not bulk send until consent, unsubscribe handling, sender reputation, and recipient relevance are in place.

- Streamers, YouTubers, newsletter writers, indie creators, community admins.

- Recent rebrand, launch, impersonation concern, or new channel creation.

- `creator name`
- `quick username note`

## Personalization Signals

Good:

- They recently changed handle/name.
- They announced a brand/product launch.
- They mentioned impersonation, account squatting, or fake profiles.
- They publish across multiple platforms.

Avoid:

- Referencing private personal details.
- Mentioning sensitive usernames found through a scan.
- Suggesting you found "their hidden accounts."
- Any implication of identity matching.

## CTA Rules

- Ask for interest, not a 30-minute call.
- Offer a sample report or checklist before asking for a demo.
- Include one link max.
- Manually review every email.
